## Product Section — "The FinesseOS Way"
**Headline:** One link. One node. Everything you need.
**How it works:**
1. **Paste your affiliate link** — FinesseOS identifies the program, brand, and category automatically.
2. **AI builds the intelligence package** — 10 keywords, 4 buyer personas, a marketing angle, 6 content ideas, 5 target platforms, and an FTC-compliant disclosure. In seconds.
3. **Compliance is automatic** — Every node gets a compliance scan. FTC rules, disclosure language, and platform-specific guidelines are pre-loaded.
4. **Your asset vault lives inside the node** — Drag and drop banners, images, copy, and videos. Everything for that campaign, in one place.
5. **The Intelligence Hub aggregates everything** — See all your keywords, personas, and strategies across every campaign from a single view.
